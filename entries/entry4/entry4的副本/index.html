EAR DETAILS

  • Big Ears: Steady and cautious with a clear mind
  • Small ears: emotional and impulsive
  • Thick Ear: Thick ear with meaty flesh, good source of wealth.
  • Thin ear: a thin ear with meaty flesh will not gather wealth.
  • Thick earlobes: earlobes are fat and rich.
