Introducing your eye type

Dragon-eyes
The Dragon Eyed type refers to people whose eyes are bright, like the eyes of a dragon. These people are usually more confident and independent.

Elephant-eyes
The Elephant Eyed type has larger eyes and looks very beautiful. These people are usually more optimistic and adaptable.

Snake-eyes
Snake-eyed types have flat shaped eyes with a narrow center and a very cold look. They are usually opinionated and have sensitive personality.
