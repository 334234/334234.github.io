In traditional Chinese physiognomy, the shape of your lips represents your personality and desires. It can even tell if a person likes to lie.


Bow-shaped lips have a distinct personality and are clear about their goals.
Downward-turned lips are sensitive and relatively reserved, with a strong sense of empathy.


People with full lips are natural and attractive, with good connections.
People with heart shaped lips are curious and lively, like to have fun and have the potential to be leaders.
